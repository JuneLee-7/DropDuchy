Drop Duchy is HERE - And Players Are Loving It!

Our innovative Tetris-inspired strategy roguelike makes a splash on launch day!

The moment we've all been waiting for has arrived! Drop Duchy officially launched yesterday, and the response from players around the globe has been nothing short of incredible. We're thrilled to see so many of you diving headfirst into building your unique duchies, mastering the block-dropping chaos, and crafting powerful card strategies.

A Fresh Spin on Strategy and Puzzles

For those new to the realm, Drop Duchy combines the satisfying, line-clearing mechanics of classic Tetris with deep strategic deck-building and the ever-evolving challenges of a roguelike. Your goal? To construct a thriving duchy by strategically placing falling blocks that represent various buildings, resources, and defenses. Each run is different, thanks to a wide array of powerful cards you can collect and upgrade, and a host of enemies and bosses that will test your tactical acumen.
